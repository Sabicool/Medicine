- ğŸ’What is the acute management of cold autoimmune haemolytic anaemia?
    
    RBC transfusion as needed
    
    Plasmapheresis and trial of glucocorticoids (e.g. [[Prednisolone]])
    
- ğŸ’What is the long term management of cold autoimmune haemolytic anaemia?
