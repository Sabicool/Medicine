- 🍒What is the 3-6-9 rule and what is it used for?
    Used to remember the normal bowel calibre:
    - Small bowel: <3cm
    - Large bowel: <6cm
    - Appendix: <6mm
    - Caecum: <9cm
    Above this bowel is dilated → consider [[Bowel obstruction]] or [[Paralytic ileus]]
