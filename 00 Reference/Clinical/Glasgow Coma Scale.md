- Assess orientation with:
	- What’s your name?
	- Where are you?
	- What month is it?
- When assessing motor response, you need to ask a two-step action
	- Grasping and releasing your finger
	- Opening mouth and sticking out tongue
- Central stimulus
	- Trapezius squeeze
		- Localising = moving hand above the clavicle towards the stimulus
			- ![[Pasted image 20260117120157.png]]
		- Normal flexion/withdraws to pain = arm flexion with rapid movement away from the stimulus
		- Abnormal flexion = arm flexes slowly and comes across the body
			- ![[Pasted image 20260117120344.png]]
		- Extension = elbow extension
	- Supraorbital notch
