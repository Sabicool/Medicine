# Catechol Ring
- A 6-carbon benzene ring with **hydroxyl (–OH) groups at positions 3 and 4** (3,4-dihydroxybenzene)
- Without those two –OH groups, it's just phenol; with only one –OH it's still phenol
- The two hydroxyl groups are critical for receptor affinity (see below)
