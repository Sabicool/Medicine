# PV Loops
![[Pasted image 20260425172609.png]]
- External work is the work of ejecting blood from the heart and is given by:
$$
\begin{align*}
W_{\text{external}} & =\int P \cdot dV+KE_{\text{ejected blood}} \\
 & \approx \int P \cdot dV & (KE_{\text{ejected blood}}\approx 0 \text{ for heart at rest})
\end{align*}
$$
