## Echocardiographic Features of Tamponade
- Echocardiographic features may preced clinical symptoms
- Echo findings include:
	- Moderate to large pericardial fluid
	- RA early systolic collapse
	- RV early diastolic collapse
	- Lack of respiratory variation in IVC and hepatic veins
	- Bowing of IV septum into the LV
