# STEMI
## Management
- Morphine
- Oxygen
- Nitrates
- Antiplatelets
- Save tissue
- Heparin

For possible STEMI or other high-risk ACS with haemodynamic compromise, consider nursing patient in the resuscitation area and/or attaching patient to a defibrillator.
