# Investigations
## Chest-Xray
- Alveolar oedema ('batwing' appearance)
- Kerly B lines (1-2cm horizontal, peripheral engorged subpleural lymphatics) and septal lines
- Cardiomegaly
- Upper love diversion
- Pleural effusion
