# Management
## TAVR vs SAVR
 - NEJM review article: [Transcatheter or Surgical Aortic-Valve Replacement in Asymptomatic Severe Aortic Stenosis](https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMclde2416168)
	 - TAVR: less invasive nature, faster recovery, and evidence from contemporary trials  demonstrating noninferiority to surgery in low-risk patients
	 - SAVR: long-term durability, concerns raised about late stroke and valve thrombosis in TAVR, and the absence of robust data beyond 10 years for TAVR in low-risk populations
	- In the absence of definitive long-term comparative data in asymptomatic, low-risk patients, the choice between TAVR and SAVR should be individualized.
