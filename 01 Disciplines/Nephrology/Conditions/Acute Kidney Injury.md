# Definitions
- KDIGO AKI workgroup defined AKI as any of the following:
	- ↑ Cr by 26.5 µmol/L
	- ↑ Cr to ≥ 1.5 x baseline
	- Urine output < 0.5 mL/kg/h for 6 hours
