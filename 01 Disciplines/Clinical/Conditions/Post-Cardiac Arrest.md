![[consequences of post-cardiac arrest syndrome.jpg]]

- Endothelial response to prolonged hypoxia is pro-inflammatory mimicking [[Sepsis|Septic Shock]]; the hypotension is similarly responsive to noradrenaline
- There is post-cardiac arrest myocardial stunning for 48-72 hours during which period the heart is responsive to inotropes
